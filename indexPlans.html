Our Training Philosophy

Nike's training philosophy is built on a foundation of innovation, performance, and inspiration. The company believes that through innovative product design, training programs, and community support, athletes can push their limits and achieve their full potential. Nike's training philosophy is based on the idea that training is more than just a physical activity - it's a mindset and a lifestyle.

“You need to celebrate your journey to the starting line, and one of the easiest things to celebrate will be your progression as an athlete.”

Chris Bennett
Nike Running Global Head Coach

At the heart of Nike's training philosophy is a commitment to innovation. The company is constantly developing new products and technologies to help athletes perform at their best. This includes everything from cutting-edge training gear to digital tools and apps that help athletes track their progress and stay motivated.
